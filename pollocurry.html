CURRY CHICKEN

INGREDIENTS:

  • chicken(breast or thight)
  • olive oil
  • onion
  • garlic
  • curry powder
  • coconut milk
  • greek yogurt
  • salt,pepper and red chili pepper

HOW TO MAKE:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook onion and garlic until it becomes golden.
  2. cook the chicken in same pan, after the chicken is cooked add coconut milk and greek yogurt.
  3. after the chicken is cooked add coconut milk and greek yogurt.
  4. add curry powder, salt, pepper and red chili pepper.
  5. reduce the heat and cook it for 30 minutes.
  6. serve it!

PASTA AGLIO OLIO PEPERONCINO

INGREDIENTS:

  • spaghetti
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • red chili peppers
Optional:
  • parsley
  • parmesan cheese

HOW TO MAKE:

  1. salt the water and cook spaghetti.
  2. take a pan and cook the garlic with olive oil and red chili peppers until the garlic is golden.
  3. when the pasta is coocked, transfer the pasta in the pan with the ingredients.
  4. taste, and add seasoning if needed.
  5. serve it!
